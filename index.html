CNC
Bending

Computer numerically controlled (CNC) Bending is a manufacturing process that is carried out by CNC press brakes (also known as CNC brake presses). These machines can bend sheet metal work from just a few mm across to sections many metres long on the largest industrial machines. We provide CNC Bending of 3m span with 10mm thick bending.

  • In addition to this, we have now introduced Laser cutting & CNC bending works. We provide Laser cutting upto 2500*6100*25MM thick & CNC Bending upto 3m span with 10mm thick bending.
