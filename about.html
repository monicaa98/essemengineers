About Factory

  • Essem Engineers & Fabricators was founded in 2002 & is a leading manufacturer, trader, supplier of Iron & Steel products. We manufacture material handeling equipments, trolleys, racks, pallets & all types of S.S/M.S Fabrication works.

  • In addition to this, we have now introduced Laser cutting & CNC bending works. We provide Laser cutting upto 2500*6100*25MM thick & CNC Bending upto 3m span with 10mm thick bending.

  • We have the workspace of 5500 sq. ft. shed, fully constructed with TRIMIX flooring with power supply of 50KW 3 phase connection.

  • We have achieved expertise in catering to the requirements of our clients exactly as per their needs. We emphasize stringent quality standards to ensure that the products provided to our clients are always optimum to their level.
